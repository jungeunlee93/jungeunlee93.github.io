About me

I'm Jungeun Lee and a Ph.D student at Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH). I am interested in Haptics, Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR). My research goal is to present enriched information to users through designing haptic hardware and rendering methods.
Specifically, my research focuses on:

  1. Design Haptic Hardware
  2. Multimodal Haptic Rendering
  3. Interaction Methods between Humans and Systems
